TATE GALLERY: A Metadata Exploration

Welcome to an immersive and interactive visualization project that brings the art world closer to you. This project dives into the metadata of approximately 3,500 artists whose art has graced the esteemed ARTIST ROOMS - a shared collection between Tate and the National Galleries of Scotland (1947 - 2004).
